# Carolina Henao Fernandez
*Researching multimodal AI for decision-making under uncertainty.*

## Current

My current research explores how multimodal AI can support evidence-aware predictive inference for art valuation.

The project investigates how visual, textual and structured auction data can be integrated into a multimodal framework capable of estimating market outcomes while explicitly modelling predictive uncertainty and evidential completeness.

Although art valuation is my current application domain, my broader research interests lie in developing AI systems that reason from incomplete and heterogeneous evidence to support reliable decision-making under uncertainty.


## Vision

Many important decisions are made under uncertainty, relying on incomplete, heterogeneous and imperfect information.

My long-term goal is to develop AI systems that reason under uncertainty, quantify the strength of available evidence and support expert judgement rather than replace it.

I believe these methods have applications far beyond the art market, including finance, healthcare, scientific discovery and other domains where understanding uncertainty is as important as making accurate predictions.
